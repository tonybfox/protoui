Export

Unlike other prototyping and mockup tools, with Proto{UI}, you can export your layouts. You can export your layout files whenever you like. This means you can try out one app framework and then easily convert it to try another without going back to the start of the build process.

Currently you can export to:-

  • Titanium Alloy
  • Appcelerator
  • Native Android
  • PhoneGap HTML
  • Ionic Framework
  • HTML 5
  • Png
